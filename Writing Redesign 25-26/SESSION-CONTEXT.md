### Key Decisions Made

**1. Leeruitkomsten Format**
- Uses **V1: Taalvaardigheid** (vakinhoudelijke leeruitkomst)
- Format: "In deze cursus wordt gewerkt aan leeruitkomst V1: Taalvaardigheid." (NL)
- English: "This course contributes to learning outcome V1: Language Proficiency."
- Source: LU Engels Handleiding confirms V1 = Taalvaardigheid under "Beheersen van het vak"

**2. Kennisbasis References**
- ONLY use **2.2 Productieve vaardigheden (2.2.1 + 2.2.2)** from concept kennisbasis MVT
- Dropped all old 2017 references (1.2 Leesvaardigheid, 2.2 Grammatica, 2.3 Lexicale kennis)

**3. AI Policy**
- **Leerproces:**
  - W1: Toegestaan met randvoorwaarden (feedback only on own writing)
  - W2: Toegestaan met randvoorwaarden (can polish, but student remains author)
  - W3: Split (timed test prep = feedback only; take-home = polishing allowed)
- **Toetsing:**
  - Timed tests (W1, W3): Niet toegestaan
  - Take-home (W2, W3): Toegestaan met randvoorwaarden (validated via P+Q&A)

**4. Concentric Structure**
- W3 exempts W1 AND W2 (has both timed test and P+Q&A at higher level)
- W2 does NOT exempt W1 (no timed test, can't validate controlled writing skills)
- Each course explains this relationship in Cursusinhoud section

**5. W3 Take-Home = Proposal Only**
- Creative option (Murderless Mystery / Personal Essay) removed
- W3 take-home is now exclusively the Proposal (formal, argumentative, professional audience)

**6. Specificity Kept Low in ACO**
- No specific durations (90 min, 20 min), word counts, or number of texts in ACO
- No Cambridge/essay references — kept generic throughout
- Rationale: Avoid over-committing to details that may change; leave for syllabus/Canvas

## 🔑 Key Course Design Elements

### Writing 1 (Schrijfvaardigheid 1)
- **CEFR Level:** B1/B2
- **EC:** 5
- **Category:** P (Propedeuse)
- **Assessment:** Timed test only (no aids)
- **No take-home component** (design decision from team meeting)
- **Concentric:** Can only be exempted by W3

### Writing 2 (Schrijfvaardigheid 2)
- **CEFR Level:** B2/C1
- **EC:** 5
- **Category:** H1 (Hoofdfase 1)
- **Assessment:** Take-home (creative mimicry) + P+Q&A
- **Grammar element:** Students highlight 3 structures (simple/medium/complex) to explain
- **No timed test** (language level validated in W1 and W3)
- **Concentric:** NOT concentric (does not exempt W1)

### Writing 3 (Schrijfvaardigheid 3)
- **CEFR Level:** C1/C2
- **EC:** 5
- **Category:** H2 (Hoofdfase 2)
- **Assessment:** Two components (50/50 weighting)
  1. Timed test (no aids)
  2. Take-home (Proposal) + P+Q&A
- **Concentric:** YES (exempts both W1 and W2)

## 🎨 Design Philosophy Summary

### Dual-Lane Strategy
- **Lane 1 (Secured):** Timed test and/or P+Q&A validate language level, authorship, and strategy awareness
- **Lane 2 (Unsecured):** Take-home product demonstrates strategy application; assessed indirectly through P+Q&A defense

### Key Principles
- APA referencing removed from all courses
- Grammar implicit (W1, W3) or explicit with awareness focus (W2)
- Timed tests follow Cambridge conventions at appropriate level (but not mentioned in cursusinhoud)
- Quality declaration form (W2, W3) puts responsibility on student
- P+Q&A validates authorship and process for take-home work
- AI policy = assessment format (built into the design)
