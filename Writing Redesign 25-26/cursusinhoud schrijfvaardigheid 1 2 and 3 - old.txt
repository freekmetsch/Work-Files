Cursusinhoud
Deze module werkt aan niveau 1 van vakinhoudelijke leeruitkomst schrijfvaardigheid: beheersing van het Engels in geschreven vorm op niveau Engels-ERK B1/B2.
In deze cursus werk je aan het verbeteren van jouw Engelse schrijfvaardigheid door middel van verschillende schriftelijke opdrachten, groepswerk en (peer) feedback.

Leeractiviteiten
Deze cursus heeft 6 lesweken waarin je zal werken aan de ontwikkeling van je schriftelijke taalvaardigheid Engels door middel van verschillende schrijf-activiteiten, klassen-discussies en uiteenlopende vormen van feedback (klassen-, zelf-, peer- en docent-feedback)

Leerdoelen
Aan het eind van de cursus:
kan je informatie uit enkele, aangereikte geschreven bronnen samenvatten en hiervan verslag doen.
kan je een structuur aanbrengen in stilistisch adequaat geschreven tekstsoorten, zodanig dat de lezer in staat is de belangrijkste punten in zich op te nemen.
kan je een beschouwende tekst op het eigen vakgebied schrijven, gebruikmakend van het APA verwijssysteem.
beheers je het geschreven Engels op niveau B1/B2 volgens het ERK.

Cursusdoelen 1 t/m 3 zijn gebaseerd op “subdomein 1.4 Schrijfvaardigheid” van de Kennisbasis Engels, versie september 2017, ingangsdatum studiejaar 2018-2019. Cursusdoel 4 verwijst naar de volgende kwalitatieve niveau criteria voor ERK C1/C2, ook terug te vinden in het specificatiemodel.

Bron: North, B., Goodier, T. and Piccardo, E., et. al. (February 2018). CEFR Companion Volume with New Descriptors. Council of Europe. Retrieved from www.coe.int/lang-cefr.

Can vary formulation to avoid frequent repetition, but lexical gaps can still cause hesitation and circumlocution. (Vocabulary range, B2-)

Grammatical correctness:
Communicates with reasonable accuracy in familiar contexts; generally good control though with noticeable mother tongue influence. Errors occur, but it is clear what he/she is trying to express. (Grammatical accuracy, B1+)

AI
AI toegestaan met randvoorwaarden: AI mag gebruikt worden om de eigen teksten te controleren op simpele taalverzorging, zoals punctuatie of spelling. AI is ook toegestaan voor brainstormen van ideeen.

AI niet toegestaan: Complexere taalverzorging zoals het herformuleren van zinnen is niet toegestaan. AI mag ook niet gebruikt worden voor het genereren van volledige zinnen, paragrafen of teksten, noch voor het genereren van bronnen of bronvermeldingen.

Onderwerp:
Can write clear, detailed texts on a variety of subjects related to his/her field of interest, synthesising and evaluating information and arguments from a number of sources. (Written production, Overall written production, B2)
Can write an essay or report that develops an argument systematically with appropriate highlighting of significant points and relevant supporting detail. (Written production, Written reports and essays, B2)
Can expand and support points of view at some length with subsidiary points, reasons and relevant examples. (Written production, Written reports and essays, C1)
Can write clear, well-structured texts of complex subjects, underlining the relevant salient issues, expanding and supporting points of view at some length with subsidiary points, reasons and relevant examples, and rounding off with an appropriate conclusion. (Written production, Overall written production, C1)
Creatief schrijven
Can write clear, detailed descriptions of real or imaginary events and experiences marking the relationship between ideas in clear connected text, and following established conventions of the genre concerned. (Written production, Creative writing, B2)
Can incorporate idiom and humour, though use of the latter is not always appropriate. (Written production, Creative Writing, C1)
Woordenschat:
Can select from several vocabulary options in almost all situations by exploiting synonyms of even less common words. (Vocabulary range, C1)
Has a good command of common idiomatic expressions and colloquialisms; can play with words fairly well. (Vocabulary range, C1)
Uses less common vocabulary idiomatically and appropriately. (Vocabulary control, C1)
Occasional minor slips, but no significant vocabulary errors. (Vocabulary control, C1)
Woordgebruik en zinsbouw:
Shows good control of elementary vocabulary but major errors still occur when expressing more complex thoughts or handling unfamiliar topics and situations. (Vocabulary control, B1+)
Lexical accuracy is generally high, though some confusion and incorrect word choice does occur without hindering communication. (Vocabulary control, B2),
Grammaticale correctheid:
Shows a relatively high degree of grammatical control. Does not make mistakes which lead to misunderstanding. (Grammatical accuracy, B2)
Good grammatical control. Occasional ‘slips’ or non-systematic errors and minor flaws in sentence structure may still occur, but they are rare and can often be corrected in retrospect. (Grammatical accuracy, B2+)
Vloeiendheid:
Can express him/herself clearly and without much sign of having to restrict what he/she wants to say. (General linguistic range, B2)
Can use a broad range of complex grammatical structures appropriately and with considerable flexibility. (General linguistic range, C1)
Can select an appropriate formulation from a broad range of language to express him/herself clearly, without having to restrict what he/she wants to say. (General linguistic range, C1)
Coherentie:
Can produce well-organised, coherent text, using a variety of cohesive devices and organisational patterns. (Coherence and cohesion, C1)
Can, when preparing a more formal spoken or written text, consciously adopt the conventions linked to the particular type
of text concerned (structure, level of formality and other conventions). (Production strategies, Planning, C1)
