# Schrijfvaardigheid 1 - BEZEM cursus / Writing Skills 1 - BEZEM Course

## Cursusinhoud / Course Content

Deze module werkt aan niveau 1 van vakinhoudelijke leeruitkomst schrijfvaardigheid: beheersing van het Engels in geschreven vorm op niveau Engels-ERK B1/B2. In deze cursus werk je aan het verbeteren van jouw Engelse schrijfvaardigheid door middel van verschillende schriftelijke opdrachten en (peer) feedback.

This module addresses level 1 of the subject-specific learning outcome for writing skills: command of written English at CEFR B1/B2 level. In this course you work on improving your English writing skills through various written assignments and (peer) feedback.

## Leeractiviteiten / Learning Activities

**Let op: dit is een BEZEM cursus. Er worden geen lessen verzorgd. Je werkt zelfstandig aan de toetsopdracht.**

**Please note: this is a BEZEM course. No classes are offered. You work independently on the assessment assignment.**

## Leerdoelen / Learning Objectives

Aan het eind van de cursus: / At the end of the course:

- kan je informatie uit enkele, aangereikte geschreven bronnen samenvatten en hiervan verslag doen. / you can summarise information from several provided written sources and report on them.
- kan je een structuur aanbrengen in stilistisch adequaat geschreven tekstsoorten, zodanig dat de lezer in staat is de belangrijkste punten in zich op te nemen. / you can apply structure to stylistically appropriate written text types, enabling the reader to absorb the main points.
- kan je een beschouwende tekst op het eigen vakgebied schrijven, gebruikmakend van het APA verwijssysteem. / you can write a discursive text in your own subject area, using the APA referencing system.
- beheers je het geschreven Engels op niveau B1/B2 volgens het ERK. / you have command of written English at B1/B2 level according to the CEFR.

Cursusdoelen 1 t/m 3 zijn gebaseerd op "subdomein 1.4 Schrijfvaardigheid" van de Kennisbasis Engels, versie september 2017, ingangsdatum studiejaar 2018-2019. Cursusdoel 4 verwijst naar de volgende kwalitatieve niveau criteria voor ERK C1/C2, ook terug te vinden in het specificatiemodel.

Course objectives 1 to 3 are based on "subdomain 1.4 Writing Skills" of the English Knowledge Base, version September 2017, effective from the 2018-2019 academic year. Course objective 4 refers to the following qualitative level criteria for CEFR C1/C2, also found in the specification model.

> Bron/Source: North, B., Goodier, T. and Piccardo, E., et. al. (February 2018). *CEFR Companion Volume with New Descriptors*. Council of Europe. Retrieved from www.coe.int/lang-cefr.

**Subject:**
- Can write short, simple essays on topics of interest. *(Written production, Written reports and essays, B1+)*
- Can write a text on a topical subject of personal interest, using simple language to list advantages and disadvantages, give and justify his/her opinion. *(Written production, Written reports and essays, B1+)*
- Can write clear, detailed texts on a variety of subjects related to his/her field of interest, synthesising and evaluating information and arguments from a number of sources. *(Written production, Overall written production, B2)*

**Vocabulary:**
- Has a sufficient vocabulary to express him/herself with some circumlocutions on most topics pertinent to his/her everyday life such as family, hobbies and interests, work, travel, and current events. *(Vocabulary range, B1+)*
- Can vary formulation to avoid frequent repetition, but lexical gaps can still cause hesitation and circumlocution. *(Vocabulary range, B2-)*

**Word use and sentence structure:**
- Shows good control of elementary vocabulary but major errors still occur when expressing more complex thoughts or handling unfamiliar topics and situations. *(Vocabulary control, B1+)*
- Lexical accuracy is generally high, though some confusion and incorrect word choice does occur without hindering communication. *(Vocabulary control, B2)*

**Grammatical correctness:**
- Communicates with reasonable accuracy in familiar contexts; generally good control though with noticeable mother tongue influence. Errors occur, but it is clear what he/she is trying to express. *(Grammatical accuracy, B1+)*

**Fluency:**
- Has a sufficient range of language to describe unpredictable situations, explain the main points in an idea or problem with reasonable precision and express thoughts on abstract or cultural topics such as music and films. *(General linguistic range, B1+)*

**Coherence:**
- Can work out how to communicate the main point(s) he/she wants to get across, exploiting any resources available and limiting the message to what he/she can recall or find the means to express. *(Production strategies, Planning, B1-)*

## Toetsing / Assessment

De cursus wordt afgesloten met het schrijven van een individueel beschouwend essay. Daarnaast vindt er een auteurschapsgesprek plaats waarin je het ingeleverde werk toelicht en aantoont dat het van jouw hand is.

The course is concluded by writing an individual discursive essay. In addition, an authorship interview takes place in which you discuss your submitted work and demonstrate that it is your own.

Kennisbasis Engels / English Knowledge Base — Domein 1 / Domain 1: Taalvaardigheden / Language Skills
- 1.2 Leesvaardigheid / Reading Skills
- 1.4 Schrijfvaardigheid / Writing Skills

Kennisbasis ICT / ICT Knowledge Base: 1.3, 1.5, 1.6, 1.7, 2.1, 2.2, 2.3, 3.1.2

Dublin descriptoren / Dublin Descriptors:
- Kennis en inzicht / Knowledge and Understanding
- Toepassen van kennis en inzicht / Applying Knowledge and Understanding

## Leeruitkomsten / Learning Outcomes

Schrijfvaardigheid 1 is onderdeel van de Leeruitkomst V1: Taalvaardigheid - Beheersen van het vak. Schrijfvaardigheid 1 valt onder de bekwaamheidseis "Vakinhoudelijk bekwaam".

Writing Skills 1 is part of Learning Outcome V1: Language Proficiency - Mastering the Subject. Writing Skills 1 falls under the competency requirement "Subject-content competent".

## Artificial Intelligence (AI) indicatoren / AI Indicators

**AI toegestaan met randvoorwaarden:** AI mag gebruikt worden om de eigen teksten te controleren op simpele taalverzorging, zoals punctuatie of spelling. AI is ook toegestaan voor brainstormen van ideeen.

**AI permitted with conditions:** AI may be used to check your own texts for basic language accuracy, such as punctuation or spelling. AI is also permitted for brainstorming ideas.

**AI niet toegestaan:** Complexere taalverzorging zoals het herformuleren van zinnen is niet toegestaan. AI mag ook niet gebruikt worden voor het genereren van volledige zinnen, paragrafen of teksten, noch voor het genereren van bronnen of bronvermeldingen.

**AI not permitted:** More complex language editing such as rephrasing sentences is not permitted. AI may also not be used for generating complete sentences, paragraphs or texts, nor for generating sources or references.

# Schrijfvaardigheid 2 - BEZEM cursus / Writing Skills 2 - BEZEM Course

## Cursusinhoud / Course Content

Deze module werkt aan niveau 2 van vakinhoudelijke leeruitkomst schrijfvaardigheid: beheersing van het Engels in geschreven vorm op niveau Engels-ERK B2/C1. In deze cursus werk je aan het verbeteren van jouw Engelse schrijfvaardigheid door middel van verschillende schriftelijke opdrachten en (peer) feedback.

This module addresses level 2 of the subject-specific learning outcome for writing skills: command of written English at CEFR B2/C1 level. In this course you work on improving your English writing skills through various written assignments and (peer) feedback.

## Leeractiviteiten / Learning Activities

**Let op: dit is een BEZEM cursus. Er worden geen lessen verzorgd. Je werkt zelfstandig aan de toetsopdracht.**

**Please note: this is a BEZEM course. No classes are offered. You work independently on the assessment assignment.**

## Leerdoelen / Learning Objectives

Aan het eind van de cursus: / At the end of the course:

- kan je informatie uit enkele aangereikte geschreven bronnen samenvatten en hiervan samenhangend verslag doen. / you can summarise information from several provided written sources and report on them coherently.
- kan je een structuur aanbrengen in verschillende, stilistische adequaat geschreven tekstsoorten, zodanig dat de lezer in staat is de belangrijkste punten in zich op te nemen. / you can apply structure to various stylistically appropriate written text types, enabling the reader to absorb the main points.
- kan je een vergelijkende tekst schrijven, gebruikmakend van het APA verwijssysteem. / you can write a comparative text using the APA referencing system.
- beheers je het geschreven Engels op niveau B2/C1 volgens het ERK. / you have command of written English at B2/C1 level according to the CEFR.

Doelen 1 t/m 3 zijn gebaseerd op "subdomein 1.4 Schrijfvaardigheid" van de Kennisbasis Engels, versie september 2017, ingangsdatum studiejaar 2018-2019. Doel 4 is gebaseerd op het ERK.

Objectives 1 to 3 are based on "subdomain 1.4 Writing Skills" of the English Knowledge Base, version September 2017, effective from the 2018-2019 academic year. Objective 4 is based on the CEFR.

De leeruitkomsten verwijzen naar de volgende kwalitatieve niveau criteria voor ERK C1/C2, ook terug te vinden in het specificatiemodel. / The learning outcomes refer to the following qualitative level criteria for CEFR C1/C2, also found in the specification model.

> Bron/Source: North, B., Goodier, T. and Piccardo, E., et. al. (February 2018). *CEFR Companion Volume with New Descriptors*. Council of Europe. Retrieved from www.coe.int/lang-cefr.

**Subject / Onderwerp:**
- Can write clear, detailed texts on a variety of subjects related to his/her field of interest, synthesising and evaluating information and arguments from a number of sources. *(Written production, Overall written production, B2)*
- Can write an essay or report that develops an argument systematically with appropriate highlighting of significant points and relevant supporting detail. *(Written production, Written reports and essays, B2)*
- Can expand and support points of view at some length with subsidiary points, reasons and relevant examples. *(Written production, Written reports and essays, C1)*
- Can write clear, well-structured texts of complex subjects, underlining the relevant salient issues, expanding and supporting points of view at some length with subsidiary points, reasons and relevant examples, and rounding off with an appropriate conclusion. *(Written production, Overall written production, C1)*

**Creative writing / Creatief schrijven:**
- Can write clear, detailed descriptions of real or imaginary events and experiences marking the relationship between ideas in clear connected text, and following established conventions of the genre concerned. *(Written production, Creative writing, B2)*
- Can incorporate idiom and humour, though use of the latter is not always appropriate. *(Written production, Creative Writing, C1)*

**Vocabulary / Woordenschat:**
- Can select from several vocabulary options in almost all situations by exploiting synonyms of even less common words. *(Vocabulary range, C1)*
- Has a good command of common idiomatic expressions and colloquialisms; can play with words fairly well. *(Vocabulary range, C1)*
- Uses less common vocabulary idiomatically and appropriately. *(Vocabulary control, C1)*
- Occasional minor slips, but no significant vocabulary errors. *(Vocabulary control, C1)*

**Word use and sentence structure / Woordgebruik en zinsbouw:**
- Shows good control of elementary vocabulary but major errors still occur when expressing more complex thoughts or handling unfamiliar topics and situations. *(Vocabulary control, B1+)*
- Lexical accuracy is generally high, though some confusion and incorrect word choice does occur without hindering communication. *(Vocabulary control, B2)*

**Grammatical correctness / Grammaticale correctheid:**
- Shows a relatively high degree of grammatical control. Does not make mistakes which lead to misunderstanding. *(Grammatical accuracy, B2)*
- Good grammatical control. Occasional 'slips' or non-systematic errors and minor flaws in sentence structure may still occur, but they are rare and can often be corrected in retrospect. *(Grammatical accuracy, B2+)*

**Fluency / Vloeiendheid:**
- Can express him/herself clearly and without much sign of having to restrict what he/she wants to say. *(General linguistic range, B2)*
- Can use a broad range of complex grammatical structures appropriately and with considerable flexibility. *(General linguistic range, C1)*
- Can select an appropriate formulation from a broad range of language to express him/herself clearly, without having to restrict what he/she wants to say. *(General linguistic range, C1)*

**Coherence / Coherentie:**
- Can produce well-organised, coherent text, using a variety of cohesive devices and organisational patterns. *(Coherence and cohesion, C1)*
- Can, when preparing a more formal spoken or written text, consciously adopt the conventions linked to the particular type of text concerned (structure, level of formality and other conventions). *(Production strategies, Planning, C1)*
