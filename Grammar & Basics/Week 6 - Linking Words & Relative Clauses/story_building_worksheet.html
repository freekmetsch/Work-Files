Two park staff members struggle to secure the building as raptors breach the visitor center.
Main Clauses
The ranger slammed the emergency door shut.
The scientist checked the control screen for security breaches.
The raptors screeched from the hallway.
The security door groaned under pressure.
The ranger shoved their weight against the security door.
The scientist triggered the alert on the control screen.

The control screen flashed a red override signal.

who had survived the first breakout

that was meant to keep the raptors out
that displayed the perimeter locks
that nobody had inspected that morning
